# Features

## Technical Filters

- Price near Long MA
- Golden Cross
- Golden Cross Age
- Pre-cross Validation
- Optional Long MA decline-to-rise transition
- Current Close At or Above Short MA
- Close At or Above Golden Cross Price
- Optional Pre-Cross Trough Validation
- 52 Week Distance

## Dashboard

- Scanner
- Results
- Stock Details
- Included `stock_symbols.csv` universe
- Configurable number of symbols to analyse
- Adjustable or actual-price market data selection
- Formatted scan results with a scan timestamp
- Clickable result rows with one-year stock charts
- Retried and cached Yahoo fundamental-data retrieval
- Selected-stock weighted and median industry P/E benchmarks with peer count

## Charts

- Candlestick
- MA50
- MA200
- Golden Cross Marker

## Export

- Excel
